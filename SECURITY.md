## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our software seriously. If you suspect you have found a vulnerability, please do not file a public issue.

### How to Report

Please send an email to security@example.com with the full details, including steps to reproduce the issue. 

### Timeline

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and aim to resolve the issue within 30 days.

### Bounty

We currently do not offer a bug bounty program.
